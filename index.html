Welcome to Mia's Place! Where fun meets art.

Who we are

We're a small business owned- and created by a couple that loves all things art. Our mission is to help give local artists more exposure by hosting events specifically created for them, by them! We believe that in order to bring people closer together, we need to show the beauty within us.


“Art is a means of union among men, joining them together in the same feelings, and indispensable for the life and progress toward well-being of individuals and of humanity.”

Leo Tolstoy
